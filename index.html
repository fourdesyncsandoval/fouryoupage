About

Four was a young man with no known past, raised in a facility without biological parents, and worked as a mechanic in Los Santos, drifting through life like a shadow. One day, a mysterious woman named Chaste approached him, offering him a chance to join a unique family called Desync, a group of 18 siblings who were not bound by blood but by choice. Though initially hesitant, Four was drawn to the idea of belonging and decided to meet the Desync family, where he was welcomed with open arms and quickly found his place among them. In Desync, he discovered love, support, and a sense of purpose that had been missing from his life, realizing that family was more about love and choice than biology. Over time, Four grew into the person he had always longed to be, finally feeling at home with the people who truly cared for him.
